Fragment Shader Effects

4-Layer Post-Processing Stack

Layer 1

Barrel Distortion

Center-out lens distortion focal at vec2(0.5, 0.51) (model's face). Two-coefficient model: k1 = strength, k2 = strength × 0.35. Dynamic ramp: 0.15 + progress × 0.85.

Layer 2

Chromatic Aberration

RGB channel split: R at spread × 1.02, G at 1.00, B at 0.98. Subtle prismatic fringing that intensifies with scroll.

Layer 3

Vignette

Parabolic mask: uv × (1 − uv) × 16. Power curve softens with progress: pow(vig, 0.18 + progress × 0.08). Edge darken mix: 0.82 → 1.0.

Layer 4

Film Grain

GLSL noise: fract(sin(dot(uv, vec2(12.9898, 78.233))) × 43758.5453). Intensity: ±0.03 (very subtle).
